The Bus Observatory

The Bus Observatory is a public archive of real-time data on vehicle movements and status, collected from transit systems around the world. This free service is provided by the Jacobs Urban Tech Hub at Cornell Tech.

Explore a feed below to learn more our data sources, schema, and coverage. All data is licensed under a CC BY-NC 4.0 license. Please review the license terms before using this data.

New York City Transit

Our largest longitudinal data set, with more than 1 billion observations (and still growing) citywide since October 2020.

NJTransit

More than 300 million observations covering every bus route and run statewide since April 2021.
